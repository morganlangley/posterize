About

Aid in creating a beautiful space for our users to live in. As well as, saving users time and money when moving into their new homes or apartment.

  • Primary function: Makes it simple for users to put 3D paintings on their walls virtually to see how it will look.
  • Features: Allows people of all ages to be able to use our app for free, allowing the users to choose us over our competitors.
  • Demographic: Looking at the data of our personas our demographic consists of users between the ages of 18-40. Many users are college graduates or college students decorating their first home or apartment. Our personas also show professionals to be important users of Posterize. Posterize can aid in the work of interior designers saving these users time and money on hiring assistants to measure and rehang art. As well, transporting multiple different art pieces Posterize could show them on the wall before their job even begins.

How can Posterize help you?

Saving time

  • Returning art prints
  • Transporting multiple pieces of art
  • Measuring wall for art prints

Saving money

  • Buying the wrong art print size
  • Wasting command strips
  • Losing deposit for multiple nails in the wall

How it works

  • We made our app as simple as possible making it quick and easy for the users.
  • Open up our app and hit sign in, fill in your name, email, and password.
  • Once you see the landing page, click the top right of the screen (the camera)
  • Select the image you want to see on your wall.
  • Click see in AR and get creative!

The Team

Our team collaborated to create an app that aids in interior design, saving money and time for our users. Posterize is an all-inclusive app that makes seeing your posters, art, mirrors, etc on your walls without ever having to nail a hole in the wall. Posterize is reinventing the world of interior design for professionals and just the average person.
